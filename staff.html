Ranks on this server

On this server, the ranks are shown in the chat, on the left a player's name. These are all the ranks you'll see on this server:
  • User : No tag, a simple user, does not have any command access
  • Trusted : A player who has been considered trustworthy by the staff, trusted rank comes by being active on the server and following the rules, it allows the user to votekick another player when the staff is offline.
  • Trial Operator : A trusted player, on trial period to become an operator, he has limited power, and can not handle reports.
  • Operator : A staff member who can handle reports and kick and ban people
  • Moderator : A staff member who can kick, ban, handle reports and who is also checking the work of the operators & trials
  • Admin : A staff member who can administrate the server globally, and aslo checks the work of the mods & ops & trials
  • HeadAdmin : A staff member who controls all the staff online
  • Internal Affairs : A staff member who handles power abuses from the staff below them.
  • Hawk : Not to be messed with
You'll also encounter the Youtuber rank, which is available to any youtuber, upon validation by the staff.
